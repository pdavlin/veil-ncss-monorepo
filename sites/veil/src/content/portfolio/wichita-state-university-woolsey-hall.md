Woolsey Hall will be the new home of the W. Frank Barton School of Business at Wichita State University. The new $60M, 125,000 SF building contains motorized dual roller shades in all exterior classrooms and conference rooms, providing students to perfectly tailor their learning environments at all times of the day. Woolsey Hall hosted its first students in Fall 2022.
