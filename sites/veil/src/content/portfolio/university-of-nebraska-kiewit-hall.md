Kiewit Hall is the new home for the University of Nebraska's engineering and construction students. Veil designed a complete solar tracking roller shade system based on the Draper I/O network, complete with multiple rooftop solar sensors. This facility features nearly 30,000 square feet of Phifer twill (2700) fabric to give the design team independent control of interior and exterior appearance.
