The new Memphis Brooks Museum of Art (MBMA) represents Veil's second flagship art museum project, acting as a consultant to the local window treatment subcontractor to handle the design, layout, detailing, and coordination of the automated Lutron roller shade system. Veil was tasked with carefully blending constructability and performance against the detailed architectural requirements of the 112,000 sqft mass timber building, balancing key visual features with technical wiring and programming requirements of the motorized roller shades. MBMA will open its doors in 2026, stay tuned for future project updates.
